Welcome to SHIT QUEEN, where creativity meets culinary delights. Founded by Hannah, our restaurant is dedicated to providing a unique dining experience that challenges conventional norms. Join us on this journey and discover why Hannah is striving to become the SHIT QUEEN.

About Us

Hannah has always been passionate about pushing boundaries and creating unforgettable experiences. With a background in culinary arts and a knack for the unconventional, she decided to open SHIT QUEEN, a restaurant that brings humor and innovation to the dining table. Our mission is to delight and surprise our guests with dishes that are as amusing as they are delicious.

Menu

Our menu features a variety of creatively named and presented dishes, all crafted with the finest ingredients. From appetizers to desserts, every item is designed to make you smile and satisfy your taste buds. Come and try our signature dishes like "Poo-tine", "Dung Donuts", and more!
